About

Principal violinist with over 15 years of orchestral and chamber music experience. Specialising in Romantic and Contemporary repertoire, Martin brings a rare combination of technical precision and emotional depth to every performance. Graduate of the National Taiwan University of the Arts, with guest appearances at Carnegie Hall and the National Concert Hall Taipei.
