This Privacy Policy explains how OPUS.Z ("we", "us") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information when you use the OPUS.Z website and platform (the "Platform"). We aim to keep this simple and honest.

1. Introduction

OPUS.Z is a platform that connects Taiwan's top classical musicians with clients seeking performances and teaching. To make that work, we collect some information about the musicians and clients who use the Platform. By using the Platform, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information we collect

Information you give us

  • Account details — your name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Musician profile — biography, instruments, qualifications, location, photos, recordings, videos, availability, and contact details you choose to add, including any social media links (Instagram, Facebook, YouTube).
  • Client details — information about you and your event when you make an enquiry or booking.
  • Messages — the content of messages you send to other users or to us through the Platform.

Information collected automatically

  • Basic technical data such as your device type, browser, and approximate region.
  • Usage data such as the pages you view and actions you take, collected through analytics tools.

3. How we use your information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To display musician profiles publicly so that clients can discover and contact them.
  • To enable enquiries, messaging, and bookings between clients and musicians.
  • To operate, maintain, secure, and improve the Platform.
  • To send you service-related and, where you have opted in, promotional communications.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect against fraud or misuse.

4. When we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only as needed to run the Platform:

  • With other users. A musician's profile (and the public information on it) is shown to clients and visitors. When a client and musician communicate, the information each chooses to share is visible to the other.
  • With service providers who help us run the Platform — for example hosting, database, authentication, email, and analytics providers — who may process data on our behalf.
  • For legal reasons — where required by law, to enforce our terms, or to protect the rights and safety of users.

5. Cookies & analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember your preferences (such as your language choice), and understand how the Platform is used. You can control cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not work without them.

6. Where your data is stored

The Platform is built on Google Firebase (Firestore, Storage, and Authentication) and is served through Cloudflare. Your data may be stored and processed on servers operated by these providers, which may be located outside Taiwan, including in the United States. We rely on these providers' security measures together with our own to protect your information.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure. While we work to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. International users

OPUS.Z is operated from Taiwan, but the Platform can be used from the United States and elsewhere, and many of our musicians live abroad. If you access the Platform from outside Taiwan, you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed in Taiwan and in the countries where our service providers operate.

8. Your rights & choices

  • You can view and update most of your information directly in your account dashboard.
  • You can ask us to access, correct, or delete your personal information, subject to legal limits.
  • You can unsubscribe from promotional emails at any time using the link in those emails.

To make a request, contact us at info@opuszmusic.com.

9. How long we keep data

We keep your information for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the Platform, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When data is no longer needed, we delete or anonymise it. Copies may remain in backups for a reasonable period.

10. Children

The Platform is not intended for children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can remove it.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you. Your continued use of the Platform after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.

12. Contact us

Questions about your privacy or this policy? Email us at info@opuszmusic.com or visit our contact page.

本隱私政策說明 OPUS.Z（以下稱「我們」）在您使用 OPUS.Z 網站及平台（以下稱「本平台」）時，如何蒐集、使用、分享並保護您的個人資料。我們希望把這件事說得簡單而誠實。

1. 前言

OPUS.Z 是一個連結台灣頂尖古典音樂家與尋找演出、教學之業者的平台。為了讓平台運作，我們會蒐集使用平台的音樂家與業者的部分資料。當您使用本平台，即表示您同意此處所述的做法。

2. 我們蒐集的資料

您主動提供的資料

  • 帳號資料——您註冊時的姓名、電子郵件與密碼。
  • 音樂家檔案——您選擇填寫的簡介、樂器、學經歷、地區、照片、錄音、影片、檔期與聯絡方式，包含任何社群媒體連結（Instagram、Facebook、YouTube）。
  • 業者資料——您詢問或預約時，關於您本人與活動的資訊。
  • 訊息內容——您透過本平台傳送給其他使用者或傳給我們的訊息內容。

自動蒐集的資料

  • 基本技術資料，例如您的裝置類型、瀏覽器與大略所在地區。
  • 使用資料，例如您瀏覽的頁面與操作行為，透過分析工具蒐集。

3. 資料的用途

  • 建立與管理您的帳號。
  • 公開顯示音樂家檔案，讓業者能發現並聯繫他們。
  • 促成業者與音樂家之間的詢問、傳訊與預約。
  • 營運、維護、保護並改善本平台。
  • 寄送服務相關通知，以及在您同意的情形下寄送行銷訊息。
  • 遵循法律義務，並防範詐欺或濫用。

4. 何時會分享資料

我們不會販售您的個人資料。僅在營運平台所必要時才會分享：

  • 與其他使用者。音樂家的檔案（及其上的公開資訊）會顯示給業者與訪客。當業者與音樂家溝通時，雙方各自選擇分享的資訊會對另一方可見。
  • 與服務供應商。協助我們營運平台者——例如代管、資料庫、身分驗證、電子郵件與分析服務商——可能代我們處理資料。
  • 基於法律原因。於法律要求、執行我們條款，或保護使用者權利與安全之必要時。

5. Cookies 與分析

我們使用 Cookies 及類似技術，以維持您的登入狀態、記住您的偏好（例如語言選擇），並了解平台的使用情形。您可透過瀏覽器設定控制 Cookies，但部分功能在沒有 Cookies 的情況下可能無法運作。

6. 資料儲存於何處

本平台建構於 Google Firebase（Firestore、Storage 與身分驗證）之上，並透過 Cloudflare 提供服務。您的資料可能儲存及處理於這些供應商所營運的伺服器，其位置可能在台灣以外，包括美國。我們仰賴這些供應商的安全措施，並搭配我們自身的措施來保護您的資料。

沒有任何傳輸或儲存方式是百分之百安全的。我們會盡力保護您的資料，但無法保證絕對的安全。

7. 跨國使用者

OPUS.Z 由台灣營運，但本平台可於美國及其他地區使用，且我們許多音樂家居住於海外。若您自台灣以外的地區使用本平台，您了解您的資料可能會被傳輸至台灣，以及我們服務供應商所在的國家進行處理。

8. 您的權利與選擇

  • 您可直接在帳號後台檢視及更新大部分的資料。
  • 在法律限制範圍內，您可要求查閱、更正或刪除您的個人資料。
  • 您可隨時透過行銷郵件中的連結取消訂閱。

如需提出請求，請來信 info@opuszmusic.com。

9. 資料保存期間

在您的帳號有效期間，以及為提供本平台、遵循法律義務、處理爭議與執行協議所需的期間內，我們會保存您的資料。當資料不再需要時，我們會將其刪除或匿名化；備份中的副本可能於合理期間內仍會保留。

10. 兒童

本平台並非為 18 歲以下兒童設計，我們也不會在知情下蒐集其個人資料。若您認為有兒童向我們提供了資料，請與我們聯繫，以便移除。

11. 本政策的修改

我們可能不時更新本隱私政策。當有重大變更時，我們會更新上方的「最後更新」日期，並於適當情形下通知您。變更生效後您繼續使用本平台，即視為接受更新後的政策。

12. 聯絡我們

對您的隱私或本政策有任何疑問嗎？歡迎來信 info@opuszmusic.com，或前往我們的聯絡頁面。
